Loans for foreign nationals?

We also provide loans to third country nationals, not just African citizens.

Can i terminate the loan aggrement prematurely?

Of course, you can. The easiest way is to call the Customer Service of the financial institution and express your interest in the early termination of the loan agreement. They will send you the necessary documents, the final calculation, and advise you on how to proceed.

Can i change the due date on the loan agreement?

Within one calendar month, you can postpone the due date until the 28th day of the month, according to the valid business conditions of the financial institution. Request a change no later than 14 days before the payment from the Customer Service of the financial institution.
